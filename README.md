# 📊 Electronics Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built to analyze performance across electronic products, customers, and regions. This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights using data visualization.

Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing electronics sales with key KPIs including Total Sales (30.75K), Total Orders, Total Customers , and Average Order Value (1.34K), delivering actionable insights on product performance and trends.


## 🚀 Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of:
- Sales performance over time
- Product-level revenue contribution
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Category-wise sales distribution
- City-level demand trends

## 📈 Insights Generated

### 🔹 Sales Trends
- Peak sales observed in **November**, indicating seasonal demand
- Significant dip in **June–July**, suggesting low mid-year engagement

### 🔹 Product Performance
- **Dell XPS 15** is the top-selling product (~9.2K revenue)
- Premium laptops dominate overall revenue contribution

### 🔹 Category Insights
- **Laptops** contribute the highest share (~56%)
- Smartphones and Tablets form secondary revenue streams

### 🔹 Geographic Trends
- Higher product demand observed in **Shanghai**
- Slight drop in quantity in **Shenzhen**

## 💡 Recommendations

- **Focus on top products:** Boost marketing and stock for high-performing items (especially laptops) and introduce bundles to increase revenue.
- **Leverage seasonality:** Maximize sales during peak months (November) and run campaigns to improve mid-year dips.
- **Target high-demand regions:** Strengthen presence in top cities and improve strategies in low-performing areas.
- **Diversify revenue:** Promote smartphones and tablets to reduce dependency on laptops.
- **Grow customer base:** Use discounts, referrals, and campaigns to attract new customers.
- **Increase AOV:** Apply bundling and cross-selling strategies to drive higher order value.
